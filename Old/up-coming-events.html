Diecast Dragsters will be participating in Philippi's Chili Cookoff on May 30th 2015 
Join us for an afternoon of family fun for everyone! On Sunday February 8th at the Junior Community Center the Highland Adventist School will be hosting February Fun Day. We have many activities planned that the whole family can enjoy! The Drags Not Drugs Program will be having Hot Wheel Racing: bring your favorite car, $1 per entry. Trophies will be awarded. We will also have face painting, dime toss, silent auction, bake sale, concessions and more! All proceeds will go towards building a much needed gymnasium onto the Highland Adventist School. We are looking forward to this fun filled event! Doors open at 12:00 and registration will begin at 1:00 for the racing
