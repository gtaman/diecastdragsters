Drags not drugs program

Was founded March 2006 with three goals in mind,

1) To find an activity both  children and parents could   enjoy together.

2) To equip the parents and children with valuable anti drug information.

3) Encouraging better choices and hobbies as an alternative to drug use.

BEAT THE HEAT
Is a special race where the children race against a real police 
officer in uniform.  This helps to build a relationship between the two, and further   remove the fear of law enforcement  so many children have today. Viewing law enforcement as someone who helps you and not out to get you, builds trust in authority and boost self confidence..


Fire/Rescue & EMT Race

Children race their fire trucks and rescue vehicles against attending firemen and EMT’s with special prizes going to the     winners.. Again building respect and admiration for those who serve the public..
